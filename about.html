Our Story

Building Trust Since 2014

HOLYSTONE Construction Company was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to build homes that families cherish for generations. What started as a small residential construction firm has grown into a trusted name in the industry.

Under the leadership of our Chairman & M.D., Deepak Sadanand Hanchate, and Director Dhiraj Sadanand Hanchate, we have successfully delivered over 500 residential projects, each crafted with meticulous attention to detail and unwavering commitment to quality.

Today, we stand proud as specialists in residential construction, transforming architectural dreams into tangible realities that stand the test of time.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted residential construction company, known for creating homes that embody quality, innovation, and sustainability. We envision communities where every family lives in spaces designed with care and built to perfection.

Our Philosophy

We believe that a home is more than just a structure—it's where life's most precious moments unfold. Our philosophy is rooted in integrity, transparency, and client satisfaction. Every project we undertake is treated as if we were building for our own family.
